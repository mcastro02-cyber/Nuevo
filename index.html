WHO WE ARE

Founded in 2020 in the Philippines, Nuevo Green Construction Services started with specialized works like painting, waterproofing, and roofing. Our commitment to quality workmanship and customer satisfaction quickly drove our expansion into full civil and general construction services. Today, we stand as a dynamic, forward-thinking company where sustainability is at the core of every project we undertake.

Our Journey:

  1. 2020: Founding & Specialization

    Initial focus on Painting, Waterproofing, and Roofing.

  2. 2022: Expansion & Growth

    Expanded services to include general civil construction and insulation.

  3. Today: Green Commitment

    Leading projects with advanced sustainable methods and materials.
